The Revenge of Medusa

Duration: 10 weeks (2022.09-2022.12)
Role: Backend and frontend, environment design, story writing, level design
Tool: Unity, Adobe Creative Suite
Advisor: Thomas Corbett, Ricardo Washington
Our goal was to create a 2d platformer game using unity, based on a epic tale. Based on the famous greek myth of Medusa, we tried to retell the story of Medusa as a hero seeking revenge against gods, not a monstrous feminine.
